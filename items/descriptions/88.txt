Redemption

20% Heal and Shield Power
200 Health
15 Ability Haste
100% Base Mana Regen

Active - Intervention: Target an area within 5500 range. After 2.5 seconds, call down a beam of light to Restore 180 - 360 (scales with ally level) Health to allies and burn enemy champions for 10% max Health true damage (120s cooldown).

Item can be activated whilst dead. Damage and healing reduced by 50% if the target has recently been affected by another Intervention. Strength of level-scaling effects are based on the ally's level.

Cost: 2300 (700)
